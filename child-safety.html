Noto Child Safety Standards

Noto prohibits child sexual abuse and exploitation. This page summarizes the standards, reporting path, and enforcement approach used for the app's public compliance disclosures.

Effective date: April 6, 2026 App: Noto Contact: giahuyphan0110@gmail.com

Zero tolerance for CSAE

Noto prohibits any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), including content, behavior, or interactions that sexualize, exploit, groom, or endanger minors.

Prohibited behavior

  • Create, upload, store, share, request, or promote child sexual abuse material
  • Groom, exploit, or sexually endanger minors
  • Facilitate trafficking, coercion, or sexual exploitation involving minors
  • Use Noto to coordinate, encourage, or normalize abuse of children

Reporting and enforcement

If Noto becomes aware of content or behavior involving CSAE or child sexual abuse material, action may include content removal, account restriction or termination, preservation of relevant information as required by law, and reporting to appropriate authorities or reporting organizations where required.

How to report concerns

To report child safety concerns related to Noto, contact giahuyphan0110@gmail.com. Please include enough detail for review, such as the account involved, relevant dates, and a description of the concern.

Point of contact

The designated point of contact for Google Play child safety matters is giahuyphan0110@gmail.com.
