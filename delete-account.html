Account Deletion

Delete Your Noto Account

If you are signed in to Noto, delete your account from inside the app. If the app cannot finish the deletion automatically, contact giahuyphan0110@gmail.com for help.

Delete in app first Open Noto, go to your profile, then choose Delete account.

How to delete your account

  1. Open Noto and sign in to the account you want to delete.
  2. Go to your profile and choose Delete account.
  3. Confirm the deletion inside the app.
  4. If prompted for security, sign out and sign back in again before retrying deletion.

What will be deleted

  • Your Noto account profile stored with Noto cloud services
  • Cloud-synced notes and related synced media for that account
  • Shared posts, invites, push tokens, and other account-scoped cloud data
  • Local account-scoped cached data and downloaded files removed from this device during sign-out cleanup

If the app cannot finish deletion

If automatic deletion is temporarily unavailable, email giahuyphan0110@gmail.com with the subject line Noto account deletion request. Include the email address associated with your Noto account so we can help you complete the request.

What may be retained

Some limited information may be retained where required for legal, security, fraud prevention, billing, or record-keeping purposes.
