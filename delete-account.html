Delete Your Noto Account

You can request deletion of your Noto account and associated cloud data by emailing YOUR_SUPPORT_EMAIL@example.com.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Email us from the address associated with your Noto account, if possible.
  2. Use the subject line Noto account deletion request.
  3. Include any details needed to identify your account.

What Will Be Deleted

  • Account profile information stored with Noto cloud services
  • Cloud-synced notes and related synced media, where applicable
  • Shared content associated with your account, subject to legal and operational retention needs

What May Be Retained

Some limited information may be retained where required for legal, security, fraud prevention, billing, or record-keeping purposes.
