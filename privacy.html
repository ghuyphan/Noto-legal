Privacy Policy for Noto

Effective date: April 4, 2026
Contact: YOUR_SUPPORT_EMAIL@example.com

Noto is a place-linked notes and photo memories app. This Privacy Policy explains what information Noto collects, how it is used, and what choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Account information such as email address, display name, and profile photo when you sign in.
  • Notes, photos, live photo motion clips, doodles, stickers, and other content you create in the app.
  • Location data when you save a note to a place or enable nearby reminders.
  • Subscription and purchase status information needed to unlock Noto Plus features.
  • Device and app information needed for notifications, sync, diagnostics, and billing.

How We Use Information

  • To save and display your place-linked memories.
  • To remind you when you return to saved places.
  • To sync your notes and media across devices when cloud features are enabled.
  • To support sharing features with friends that you explicitly use.
  • To manage subscriptions, restore purchases, and provide customer support.

Location Data

If you enable reminders, Noto may access location data in the background so it can notify you when you return to a saved place, even when the app is closed or not in use. Background location is used for nearby note reminders.

Cloud Services

Noto may use third-party service providers such as Supabase for account, storage, sync, and sharing features, and RevenueCat and Google Play Billing for subscription management.

Sharing

If you choose to share content with friends, the shared content and related profile details may be made available to the users you select.

Your Choices

  • You can disable location permissions in your device settings.
  • You can disable notifications in your device settings.
  • You can delete local content inside the app.
  • You can request account deletion using the instructions on the Account Deletion page.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide app features, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You can request deletion of your account and associated cloud data.

Children

Noto is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law in your region.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact YOUR_SUPPORT_EMAIL@example.com.
